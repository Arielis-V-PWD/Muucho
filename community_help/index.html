1. Project Idea

App Description

A cow-themed web app showcasing recipes from various Hispanic countries explained by people from that country. Users can explore dishes by country and category—appetizers, mains, desserts, snacks, and drinks—each represented with playful cow icons (e.g., grazing cow for starters, strong bull for mains). Recipe pages include ingredient lists with regional substitutions, step-by-step instructions, cultural context, and tips from a cow mascot. The app supports both English and Spanish, with themed icons for language selection.

Target Audience

for people who ever wondered about hispanic food, want to make them, or want to learn how culture can influence food

Value Proposition

Not every hispanic in the world knows how to make their own cultural foods, this app helps people who may have never gotten the chance to learn from relatives
